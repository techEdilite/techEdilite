Our Solutions

Unite Pulse

Unite Pulse is an automated attendance management software designed to streamline and simplify workforce management for organizations. With seamless onboarding and quick integration, it effortlessly adapts to your business. Powered by AI, it offers insightful analytics and features an advanced biometric/face detection system for accurate and secure attendance tracking. Say goodbye to manual processes and embrace a smarter, more efficient way to manage employee attendance.

ConnectED

ConnectED is an app specifically designed for educational institutions, providing a centralized platform to manage user records in one place. It offers seamless communication through messaging and voice calls, backed by AI to ensure a safe and secure communication environment. With blockchain technology, ConnectED stores immutable records, maintaining privacy and ensuring data integrity. The app keeps institutions, parents, and consumers up-to-date with essential information, including records, transportation details, and attendance, offering clear and efficient updates for everyone involved.

Tech Edilite Capella

Tech Edilite Capella is a super lightweight application designed to manage inventory operations digitally. It minimizes risks, ensures rock-solid data storage for future review, and efficiently handles stock management. By reducing human errors and streamlining business operations, it provides a smarter, more effective way to track and manage inventory, making your business run smoother and more accurately. Additionally, it is partially backed by blockchain technology and cryptography, ensuring data integrity and maintaining privacy, giving you peace of mind while managing your inventory.
